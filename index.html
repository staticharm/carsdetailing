SERVICES OFFERED

Interior detailing

Car interior detailing refers to thoroughly cleaning the inside of your vehicle. We will clean the dashboard, seats, steering wheel, carpets, mats, and other interior surfaces.

Exterior detailing

It involves vacuuming, restoring, and surpassing the original condition of the exterior constituents of a vehicle, such as tires, windows and wheels, among other visible components.

PPF care

A Paint Protection Film or PPF is a clear or tinted film applied to a vehicle’s exterior surface to protect the paint from various forms of damage. It is usually made of a thin layer of Urethane, which is transparent and self-healing.

Ozone Air Purification

In the post-COVID era, every car owner needs to be aware of his environment and ensure a safe and hygienic cabin space. Unpleasant odours, bacteria, and viruses are removed by placing an Ozone Generator inside the car
